Choose your cities

Manarola, Tokyo, Rio... wherever you want to go, Adventure Buddy can recommend top hotels and accomodations. Start your adventure by selecting the cities you wish to visit, then view hotel recommendations from other users.

Pick your attractions

Once you've selected your accomodations, Adventure Buddy will recommend nearby attractions for you to go visit. We aggregate reviews from across the internet. Get advice on where to go, when to visit, how to get there and how much time to spend.

Plan your transportation

With that out of the way, all that's left is getting there. Adventure Buddy can compare the cost and time of multiple modes of transportation. So whether you drive, fly, take a ferry or hitchhike, you can be confident you'll be traveling in style.

Tell your friends

Email your itinerary to friends and family to let them know where you'll be day by day. Add your trip to our gallery and give other users inspiration for their own journeys. Or update your Adventure Buddy profile with summaries of your trips.

Balance your budget

Don't get surprised by bad exchange rates, expensive food or overpriced gas. Adventure buddy's built-in budget calculator can help you estimate the cost of meals and transportation - so you can see the world without breaking the bank.

Share your stories

Help other travelers by reviewing the places you've been. Upload photos of your adventure and let other adventurers know what they're missing. Find a cool location on your journey? Add it to our system, so that others can see it and visit as well.
